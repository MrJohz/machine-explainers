Unbreakable Cyphers - How To Build An Enigma Machine

Encryption is surprisingly easy. All you need are some random numbers.

Let's say you and your friend have a book full of thousands and thousands of random numbers, and you want to send them a message that only they can read.

All you need to do is take each letter of your message, and each random number, and add them together (or at least, move each letter through the alphabet based on the random number.)
